The Vienna

The Vienna Opening is described as an aggressive opening for white. It is characterized by 1)e4, e5 2)Nc3.

The Scotch Gambit

The Scotch Gambit is an attacking, tactical opening for white. Although it gambits a pawn early, white can usually gain the pawn back early in the game. The Scotch Gambit is characterized by 1)e4, e5 2)Nf3, Nc6 3)d4, exd4 4)Bc5.

The London System

The London System is a solid opening for white which begins with d4 (the queen's pawn) rather than e4. The London System is popular for its strong positions that white can obtain with consistency.
